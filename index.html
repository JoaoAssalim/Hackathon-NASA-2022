CLIMAGRO

An Aplication to help farmers lose less food when the climate changes, so, our idea is an aplication to analyze if the climate is good to plant, or if it is better to wait a few days to not lose your food.

Watch the Project GitHub Project

Detailed Project Description

Our Idea is create and software to analyze and tell the farmer if it's a good day to plat your food, or if forecasts say there will be frost, snow or high temperatures, so the farmer plant or wait a few days to do it. And for that, we need to analyze weather forecasts and possible changes in temperatures or possible frosts, because many foods do not support this change, and with that they end up being lost, so we have the idea of ​​an application that analyzes the area and gives feedback whether it would be a good time to plant this certain food.

Who our project helps?

Every Farmer

Helps Farmers by analyzing the weather and temperature to plant or to wait few days

Lose less Food

Avoiding food loss due to climate change and getting more out of these crops

Markets

Buyers
